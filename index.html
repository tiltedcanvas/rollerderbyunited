Welcome

We hope you're doing well and staying healthy through this pandemic. As you may know, a lot of roller derby leagues have been hit hard by COVID-19. With bouts and event-based fundraisers canceled for the foreseeable future, and with some in our community no longer able to pay dues (especially while practices are canceled indefinitely), so many leagues are hurting. Now, more than ever, our community needs to come together and lift each other up - we are all in this together, and we can make a difference through the strong community we've built!

Roller derby has been a safe space, an affirming space, a source of entertainment, and a lifeline for so many people. Now is the time to give back (if you can!!) and help make sure roller derby leagues remain open and operational in a post-COVID world. Keep scrolling for how you can contribute, and how to apply if you are a struggling league.

How you can contribute

As part of this fundraiser, we are selling rad t-shirts ($25 each plus shipping) and collecting donations. All proceeds from t-shirt sales and 100% of donations will be put into a relief fund, to be split among struggling leagues. The goal of this fund is to make up for revenue lost due to COVID-19, and help leagues stay open and operational post-pandemic. Everyone who purchases a t-shirt or donates will additionally be e-mailed a PDF of the cutest roller derby colouring pages—fell free to print at home, and colour in while practicing social distancing!

In addition to financial contributions, posting and sharing this fundraiser on your social media would be hugely beneficial. We encourage you to share our website link far and wide, post your filled in colouring pages, and talk to your friends/family about this fundraiser—the more money we raise, the more leagues we can help and the more meaningful a contribution we can distribute. Feel free to use our fundraising hashtags when posting: #RollerDerbyUnited #TheNewEngagementZone

Tshirt Design

100% of all t-shirt proceeds will go directly into the COVID-19 Roller Derby United relief fund, to be split among struggling leagues. Everyone who purchases a t-shirt or makes a donation will additionally receive a PDF of the cutest roller derby themed, at-home colouring pages via email as a thank you from us.

Donate

If you would like to donate to this fundraiser, 100% will go directly into the relief fund.

Apply for Assistance

Download the application below, fill it out with information from your league, and email the filled out document to rollerderbyunited@torontorollerderby.com with the subject line “RELIEF FUND APPLICATION”

The more money we raise, the more leagues we can help, and the more meaningful a contribution we can distribute. We encourage all leagues to share this fundraiser to your audience, so we can reach and benefit as many people as possible. We are stronger together.
