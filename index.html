Welcome

We hope you're doing well and staying healthy through this pandemic. Like a lot of leagues, Toronto Roller Derby has been hit hard by COVID-19. With bouts and event-based fundraisers canceled for the foreseeable future, and with some in our community no longer able to pay dues (especially while practices are canceled indefinitely), so many leagues are hurting. Now, more than ever, the roller derby community needs to come together and lift each other up - we are all in this together, and we can make a difference through the strong community we've built!

With this in mind, ToRD has organized a fundraiser where proceeds will be split among derby leagues in need - our goal is to prevent roller derby leagues from going under and reduce financial stress during this time. Keep reading for details on the fundraiser, how you can apply, and how you can promote the fundraiser on your channels - we could use all the support we can get!
